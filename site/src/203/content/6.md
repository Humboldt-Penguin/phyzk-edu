## [3] Recitation Prep

I recommend reviewing the following concepts, they will probably come up in recitation:

- 1D...
    - One object bouncing against something.
    - Two objects bouncing against each other (elastic/inelastic).
- 2D...
    - Elastic/inelastic collisions between two objects travelling at different speeds/directions (e.g. one North and one East, or "glancing blow" where one object hits another at an angle).
