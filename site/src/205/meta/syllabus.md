## [1] Absences & Makeup Policy

Students must complete all 10 labs for credit. However, if you miss a class, you have **two** options:

&nbsp;

#### Option 1: Attend another lab section in the same week

See bottom of page for schedule of all lab sections...

- If I teach the section (W-78, F-12, or F-34), you may attend without asking.
- If another instructor teaches the section, you MUST email them to politely ask if you can attend their section. Please CC me on the email.

??? abstract "Email template for make-up requests"

    Subject: [PHY205] Make-up Request

    &nbsp;

    Hello [INSTRUCTOR NAME],

    &nbsp;

    Is it okay if I make-up this week's lab in your section [#], on [DATE] [TIME]?

    My primary instructor is Zain Kamal (zain.eris.kamal@rutgers.edu).

    If there's a quiz this week, Zain told me I will make it up with him separately.

    &nbsp;

    Thank you,

    [YOUR NAME]

    [YOUR NETID]

    [YOUR RUID]

&nbsp;

#### Option 2: Make-up at end of semester

Usually, only ONE special make-up lab is allowed at the end of the semester.

However, please try to avoid this if possible.
